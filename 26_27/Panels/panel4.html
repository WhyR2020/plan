12:00 - 12:45 UTC+2 Geospatial Stream

rspatial is one of the examples of prolific R communities coordinating the efforts to push the boundaries of spatial data analysis in R even further. The aim of this panel is to highlight not only recent efforts of rspatial, but also shed some light on the planned endeavors. Roger Bivand, Dewey Dunnington, Tomislav Hengl, Robin Lovelace and Paula Moraga will talk about the most exciting among existing or forthcoming tools to analyze spatial data in R (27.09.2020, 12:00-12:45 PM GMT+2). You can submit your questions using WhyR.pl/slack/ till 24.09.2020.

Moderator: Robin Lovelace (University of Leeds)
Participants:
  • Roger Bivand (Norwegian School of Economics)
  • Dewey Dunnington (Dalhousie University)
  • Tomislav Hengl (OpenGeoHub Foundation)
  • Paula Moraga (King Abdullah University of Science and Technology)
