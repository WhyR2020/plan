18:30 - 19:15 UTC+2 Performance in R Stream

R has a reputation of a slow and memory-inefficient language. Is this true, or does it stem from the improper usage of the R? How can users avoid such limitations? The aim of this panel is to highlight less-known performance problems and provide a unique insight into the internal workings of R.

Moderator: Kylie Bemis (Northeastern University)
Participants:
  • Tomas Kalibera (University of Kent)
  • Jan Vitek (Northeastern University)
  • Luke Tierney (University of Iowa)
